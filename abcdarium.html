ABCDARIUM - 2017

closed: 210x297 mm
open: 420x297 mm

An ABCdarium is a book meant for children to teach them the alphabet through associations. A is for apple, B is for banana, ... This principal revisited in a new theme. The theme that I focused on was the things we find dirty/disgusting.


All pictures were taken by me and were taken with flashlight. This light exaggerated the dirt of the objects and the rooms. All the rooms, the background pictures, had to have the corner right in the middle of the spread, so when you opened it the room had some perspective illusion.
