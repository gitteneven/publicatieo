UNDER THE SKIN - 2017

315x315x3 mm

The vinyl is my take on the soundtrack of the movie Under the Skin by Jonathan Glazer. The movie had a very distinctive style and I wanted to merge this with my style. The soundtrack has very sharp and high pitch sounds, that's why I choose for these stretched letters. I wanted to show this contrast, because the movie also had these high contrasts.
